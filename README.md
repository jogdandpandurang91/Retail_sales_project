# Retail Sales Performance & Profitability Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to understand sales trends, product performance, and profitability across different regions and customer segments.
The goal of the analysis is to extract actionable business insights that can help improve sales strategies and profitability.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze sales performance across product categories
* Identify the most profitable regions and products
* Understand the impact of discounts on profitability
* Identify loss-making products
* Analyze monthly sales trends

## Key Insights

* Certain product categories contribute significantly to total revenue.
* Some regions generate higher profit compared to others.
* Higher discounts tend to reduce overall profit margins.
* A few products generate losses despite strong sales.
